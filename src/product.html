Call To Action

Unique Vacuum Slider Concept

No other digital imager offers the unique Vacuum Slider Concept (VSC). Based on smart design, printers can insert the plate in the advanced clamping system and slide the vacuum setting toward the plate edge, and they are done! No more need for special tape constructions or cutting plates to fit dedicated vacuum zones, thus, allowing the use of cut-off scraps!

5080 DPI exposure

The Xeikon ThermoFlexX solutions are unique in their use of 5080 PPI resolution, resulting in:

  • Smaller screen dots resulting in finer details
  • Cleaner mid-tones
  • Sharper text, lines, barcodes and QR codes
  • Capability to reproduce full image contrast, due to the abundance of gray levels even at high-screening levels.

Technical characteristics

Xeikon ThermoFlexX imagers are designed for smooth and productive operation. The advanced clamping system facilitates ergonomic and easy plate mounting. The standard vacuum drum and optional hybrid vacuum/magnetic drum assure accurate plate positioning and optimal exposure of flexo and letterpress plates. The integrated display enables fast, user friendly and reliable control of the imager.
